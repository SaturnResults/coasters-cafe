Welcome to

Our Little Cafe

Homemade with love, served with a smile

Tucked away at Little Triangle behind Boots, Coasters Cafe is a friendly, family-run spot in the heart of Teignmouth — instantly recognisable by our hand-painted beach hut sign and the red chairs out front.

Step inside and you'll find soft lavender walls, sea-print cushions and the smell of fresh coffee. Everything we serve is prepared on-site each day, from our homemade soups to our cakes and scones. There's always a warm welcome — and we're dog-friendly outside too.
